Who is "home transfer" for?

Moving to a new house is a big hassle, but it is also exciting. Unsure about what to take, what to donate and what to purchase for your new home? We can help you save time, money, and mistakes. 


What is included in the service?

Home transfer" specialised service includes consultation between your two homes. Our IR3 specialist will visit both your new space and your present home, discuss all about your needs, and will provide you with a complete, organized, step-by-step plan.
If you choose to do consulting only, our specialist will make recommendations and sketch designs for you on the spot.. You can also add rendering service and get us to source the items for you.
If you choose to engage us in full turn-key service, then we will take charge of the project from start to finish; including moving your existing items to the new home, refurbishing the items to suit your new color scheme, installing new signature pieces, and so on.  adjusting the steps based on practicality and your budget & time constraints.
We can also present all the processes for you in an album or website to showcase your lovely new space to your friends and family, and help you plan your housewarming party to so all your friends will envy you!
