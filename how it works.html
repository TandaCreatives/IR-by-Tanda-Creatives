I still don't get the difference between your level of services above. Which one should I choose?

Everybody's favorite is the full turn-key service, where all you have to do is to tell us what you want, close your eyes, and let us do the rest (although you are more than welcome to give your two cents along the way). However, if you like getting crafty yourself or you enjoy shopping alone, you can hire us to do consulting only so that we can provide you a clear direction for you to follow. Or, if you are already satisfied with your space and all it's lacking is just a few signature pieces that you couldn't get hold of, you can get us to source, purchase and get them delivered to your doorstep.

I want turn-key service but I need to get my house done for an event / before my baby is born. How long will the process take?

It depends on how big the space is, how thorough you want  the change to be, and so on. The process can take from a few weeks to a few months. Feel free to talk to us about your specific needs, requirements and time constraints, and we will work out a proposal that suits your time frame.
